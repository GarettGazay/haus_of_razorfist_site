Kink Education & Safety

Kink Education Code of Conduct (KECC): Ethical guidance for educators/producers, emphasizing good judgment.

Consent: Voluntary, informed agreement involving collaboration and awareness of feelings/power dynamics.

SSC (Safe, Sane, Consensual): Focuses on safety (risk mitigation), sanity (sound mind), and ongoing consent.

RACK (Risk-Aware Consensual Kink): Emphasizes informed consent to risks, inclusive of edgeplay.

PRICK (Personal Responsibility, Informed, Consensual Kink): Stresses individual accountability and education.

Rope Suspension: Riggers

Requirements: Experience, humility, safety mindset, risk management plans.

Preflight Checklist:

  • Partner Interview: Discuss health, hydration, injuries, allergies.
  • Risk Communication: Explain marks, bruising, nerve risks, equipment safety.
  • Environment Check: Inspect hardpoints, ensure safety cutters, use mats.

Matting: Use puzzle flooring or gym mats; mitigate sliding/tripping risks.

Rope Suspension: Bottoms

Preparation: Build flexibility/strength, communicate medical conditions.

Communication: Report numbness, tingling, or immobility immediately; avoid gags initially.

Safety: Start with experienced riggers, request trusted third parties if needed.

Suspension Preparation Checklist

  • Confirm rigger skill and mental/physical readiness.
  • Inspect hardpoints, ropes, and environment.
  • Ensure partner readiness.
  • Plan for issues (numbness, fainting, emergency rope cutting).
  • Plan for aftercare and cleanup.
